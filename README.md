# DangoGo_GuiAssets
Dango-Go! is an educational fast-paced cooking game where you run a small dango shop. Your goal is to serve customers as quickly as possible before their patience runs out.

You start with raw dango on a tray. Drag a raw dango into the boiling pot and wait 6 seconds for it to cook. Then click the skewer cup to get a stick and drag it onto the boiled dango to make a complete skewer. You can add toppings like Matcha, Red Bean, or Soy Sauce to earn extra money.

Each customer has a waiting bar. If the bar reaches zero, they leave angry and you get nothing. They also have specific orders. Some want plain dango, others want a specific topping. Serving the wrong topping will be rejected.

The game lasts 2 minutes. The time of day changes every 30 seconds: from 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM. At 5:00 PM the background switches to night mode.

You earn 20 yen for a plain dango and 25 yen for a topped dango. Throwing dango into the trash bin costs you 10 yen. Try to earn as much money as possible before the shop closes.

There are two levels. Level 1 is easier with slower customers and 8 total customers. Level 2 is harder with faster customers and 12 total customers.

Unlockable words appear as notifications when you serve certain dango for the first time. You can view your unlocked achievements after the game ends by clicking the present button.
